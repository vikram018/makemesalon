About Make Me Salon

A Salon Built on
Passion & Purpose

Make Me Salon was born from a deep love of beauty and a desire to bring world-class salon services to the heart of Raebareli. Founded by Jaya Singh, the salon has become a trusted name for women across the city — a place where every visit feels special.

Located at Abhay Data Complex, Madhuban Road, Satya Nagar, Nirala Nagar — we are easily accessible and always welcoming. Whether it's your everyday grooming or once-in-a-lifetime bridal preparation, we are here for every moment of your beauty journey.

Our Mission
To make every woman feel beautiful, confident, and celebrated — without compromise.
Our Vision
To be Raebareli's most loved and trusted beauty destination for every generation.
Our Values
Honesty, care, skill, and genuine warmth in every single interaction.
Our Community
Proud to serve and uplift the women of Nirala Nagar and all of Raebareli.

Meet the Visionary

The Heart Behind
Make Me Salon

Jaya Singh is the passionate mind and skilled hands behind Make Me Salon. Growing up with a deep fascination for beauty, artistry, and the transformative power of self-care, she always dreamed of creating a space where every woman could feel truly seen and celebrated.

With years of professional training and hands-on experience across hair, makeup, skincare, and bridal services, Jaya established Make Me Salon in the heart of Nirala Nagar, Raebareli — bringing big-city quality beauty services to her beloved hometown.

"Beauty is not about perfection — it is about feeling confident in your own skin. My goal has always been to help every woman who walks through my door feel exactly that."

Her Journey

Passion That Built a Salon

🎨
A Love for Artistry
From a young age, Jaya was captivated by the art of beauty — experimenting with mehendi, trying hairstyles, and exploring the magic of a well-done makeup look. This curiosity became her calling.
📚
Professional Training
Jaya invested in formal beauty education, mastering techniques in hair, makeup, skincare, and nail art — ensuring that every service at Make Me Salon meets the highest professional standards.
🏠
Serving Her Hometown
Rather than moving to a big city, Jaya chose to build her dream in Raebareli — believing that women in smaller cities deserve the same quality of beauty services as anywhere else in India.
👰
Bridal Specialist
Jaya's bridal work has become legendary in Raebareli. Her attention to detail, understanding of every bride's unique vision, and ability to bring it to life has made her the city's most trusted bridal artist.
